Featured Project

A320 Wing Structural Analysis

Structural Analysis | Numerical Modelling

Developed a computational model to analyse the structural behaviour of a tapered aircraft wing under aerodynamic loading. Implemented elliptical lift distribution and numerical integration to evaluate shear force, bending moment, and stress distribution.

Max Moment

~2.2 MN·m

Max Stress

~88 MPa

Yield

300 MPa

FoS

~3.4

Key insight: Wing taper significantly improves structural efficiency by reducing stress towards the tip while concentrating material near the root where bending loads are highest.

Airbus: Fuel Systems Design

Systems Engineering & Airworthiness Compliance

Engineered the fuel tank venting, inerting, and water management systems for an A320-sized commercial aircraft, progressing the design to a Conceptual Design Review (CDR) level in collaboration with Airbus. The project involved optimizing pipe sizing, integrating an OBIGGS for inerting, and developing a water management system to comply with EASA CS-25 safety regulations.

Mechanical Design & Performance Analysis

Collaborated with Airbus to develop an electronic taxiing system for A320-sized passenger aircraft. This involved designing and integrating an electric motor system into the main landing gear and optimizing torque transfer through a two-stage planetary gear system.
